Developer Story

CraftLauncher was created by Tevo2, a developer deeply involved in the Minecraft community. His motivation came after discovering that TL, a popular launcher, was infected with a virus — putting thousands of players at risk.

That experience sparked a strong commitment to user safety. Tevo2 saw how easily malicious software could spread and how little protection players had. Since then, he's focused on building tools that are clean, transparent, and secure.

CraftLauncher reflects that mission: no ads, no trackers, no hidden code. Just pure gameplay, built with care and respect for the community. If you've ever worried about what's running behind the scenes, you're not alone — and that's exactly why CraftLauncher exists.
