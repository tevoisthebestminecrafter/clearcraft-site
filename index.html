Developer Story

CraftLauncher was created by Tevo2, a developer who’s been deeply involved in the Minecraft community for years. His journey began with a passion for clean, secure software — and a personal experience that shaped his mission.

Tevo2 was once affected by a malicious virus hidden inside a launcher called TL. That moment sparked a deep concern for user safety and transparency. He saw firsthand how easy it was for shady software to spread, and how little protection users had when trusting unknown tools.

Since then, Tevo2 has committed to building tools that are lightweight, open, and respectful of user privacy. CraftLauncher is a reflection of that — no ads, no trackers, no hidden code. Just pure gameplay, built with care.

If you’ve ever worried about what’s running behind the scenes, you’re not alone. That’s why CraftLauncher exists — to give players a launcher they can trust.
