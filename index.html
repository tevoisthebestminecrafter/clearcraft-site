Developer Story

CraftLauncher was created by Tevo2, a developer who’s been active in the Minecraft community for years. His motivation to build a secure launcher came after discovering that TL, a popular launcher, was infected with a virus — putting thousands of players at risk.

That experience made Tevo2 realize how important it is to offer tools that are clean, transparent, and safe. He’s committed to making CraftLauncher free of ads, trackers, and hidden code — so players can enjoy Minecraft without worrying about their privacy or security.

Every line of code in CraftLauncher reflects Tevo2’s dedication to protecting users and giving them full control. If you’ve ever felt unsure about what’s running behind the scenes, CraftLauncher is here to earn your trust.
